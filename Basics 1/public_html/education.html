Mangalore has become one of the major educational cities in India. This is due to the strong educational foundation laid by forefathers of undivided South Canara. Quality educational institution in the field of Engineering, Medicine, Law, Management attract students from all over the state and country to come to Mangalore.

Some of the institutions in Mangalore and around are,

  • Canara High School Association
  • Manipal University
  • National Institute of Technology
  • KMC Medical College
  • KS Hegde Medical Academy
  • Nitte Education
