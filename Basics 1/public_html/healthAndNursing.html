Mangalore has one of the best medical facilities in south India. Manipal University medical school, A.J Institute of Medical Science, Yenepoya Medical Academy and other are few among the best hospitals in Mangalore. The top scorers in the medical entrance examination earn their admits in these institutions. The people from the nearby states of Tamil Nadu, Kerala and Andra Pradesh come to Mangalore for solutions related to medical issues.

The hospitals have all-round ambulance service in the city and other places in the vicinity. The hospitals run blood donation camps and other health awareness programs in the city of Mangalore and rural areas around the city.
