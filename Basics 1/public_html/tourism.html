Mangalore has many places in and around the city for tourism. The coastal city is mainly famous for the beach resorts and food. Fish cuisine is mostly famous because the easy availability. The city has also good transportation facilities which include railways, waterways and airways.

Few places of visit in and around Mangalore are,

  • Murudeshwar
  • Kollur
  • Kudremukh
  • Agumbe
  • Udupi
  • Goa
